At Etiquette, we are dedicated to helping students develop the essential skills they need to thrive in today's competitive job market.

Our platform provides practical tutorials, tools, and resources focused on;

  • Writing professional documents such as CVs, cover letters and emails
  • Building strong communication skills for academic and workplace success.
  • Preparing for internships and job interviews with confidence
  • Learning how to navigate the workplace including teamwork, mindset, and career growth.

We also offer a dedicated space where students can read job experiences shared by peers, learn from challenges faced on the job, and gain insights into professional life after school.

Whether you are just starting to prepare for opportunities or already stepping into the professional world, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
